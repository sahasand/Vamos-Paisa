About Me

Not a tour guide.
Just a Paisa girl who
loves her city.

This started with me showing my friend's cousin around. She told people, they told people, and suddenly I had strangers messaging me on Instagram asking if I could show them "the real Medellín." So now I do this.

I grew up in Envigado, studied tourism but hated the corporate tour thing. My abuela still makes me lunch on Sundays. My girlfriends and I go dancing every weekend. When I show you around, you're basically just tagging along with my life.

Fair warning: we'll probably run into people I know everywhere. My city's like that.

First Timer? Start Here

The Real City Tour

Not the bus tour version. We take the Metro like normal people—Paisas are obsessed with it, I'll explain why. Plaza Botero to see the fat sculptures everyone posts, but also the stories behind them. Downtown where tourists don't usually go but should. Jardín Botánico where I used to study. We end at Barefoot Park because your feet will need it. This is Medellín 101, my version.

5-6 hours Max 6 guests $100 USD
The One Everyone Asks About

Comuna 13

I know people here. Real people, not tour-guide friends. We'll skip the crowds, hit the murals that actually matter, and I'll tell you what it was like growing up when this place was different. Bring good shoes.

4 hours $45 USD
After Dark

Night Out With the Girls

We start with cocktails somewhere cute in Provenza, then hit La 70 where my friends and I go every weekend. You'll meet some of them probably. Fair warning: we dance. A lot. Heels optional but encouraged.

5 hours $55 USD
For People Who Eat

Eating Our Way Through

Markets in the morning, weird fruits you've never heard of, then we eat. Chicharrón at my spot in Envigado, the best arepas in the city (fight me), and if we time it right, lunch at my abuela's. She'll try to adopt you. Come hungry, leave stuffed.

4 hours $40 USD
Get Out of the City

Metrocable & The Forest

The cable car views are unreal—I still take photos every time. Then we hike Parque Arví where it's cool and quiet and you can actually breathe. I pack snacks, we take our time. Good reset day if the city's been a lot.

6 hours $60 USD
